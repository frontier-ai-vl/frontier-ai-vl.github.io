📊 Key Findings
  • Among open-source models, Qwen2.5-VL-32B achieves the best performance across all benchmarks.
  • Cosmos-Reason1-7B outperforms Qwen2.5-VL-7B on spatial reasoning benchmark VSI-Bench and mathematical reasoning benchmark MATH-Vision, likely due to its specialized training enhancements for reasoning capabilities. However, Cosmos-Reason1-7B shows lower performance than Qwen2.5-VL-7B on some general capability benchmarks (e.g., MMMU).
  • Frontier-A1-VL(AR SFT v2) demonstrates significantly better performance than Qwen2.5-VL-7B on the VSI-Bench dataset, attributed to the inclusion of industry-specific reasoning datasets in our training data.
